How it works
Tidy up like it's a game, not a chore

Swipe to archive

It's like Tinder, but for your photos. See each photo full-screen and swipe left on the ones you don't need — old screenshots, blurry duplicates, forgotten memes. No more endless scrolling through a messy grid.

Swipe to keep

Found a memory worth holding on to? Swipe right to keep it. Made a mistake? Undo it instantly before anything is deleted — sorting through hundreds of photos has never felt this safe or satisfying.

Rediscover memories

flicpix surfaces similar shots from the same moment so you can compare and pick the best one — and resurfaces forgotten favourites from your past so nothing good gets lost in the noise.

Clear your camera roll

Once you're done swiping, clean up your entire archive in one tap. No more deleting photos one by one — just a single satisfying confirmation, and the space is yours again.

Build daily habits

A little each day goes a long way. Turn on reminders for your daily streak challenge and let flicpix nudge you gently until your whole library is sorted.

Stay on track

Track your streak, your swipes, and the storage you've saved with a simple calendar view. Watching your progress build is half the fun.

Organize your favourites

Sort the best shots straight into albums as you go — no extra steps, no switching apps. Just swipe, tap, and your favourite memories are exactly where you want them.

Focus with smart categories

Jump straight into screenshots, favourites, recents, or "On This Day" memories. Smart categories let you focus your cleanup exactly where it's needed, instead of wading through everything at once.

Your photos stay private, always

flicpix works 100% locally on your device. Your photos never leave your iPhone — we don't store, upload, or have access to your media.
