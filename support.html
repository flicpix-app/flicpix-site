Frequently asked questions

How does flicpix work?

flicpix shows your photos one at a time, full-screen. Swipe right to keep a photo, or left to archive it. When you're done, you can clear everything you've archived in a single tap to free up storage.

Are my photos private?

Yes. flicpix works entirely on your device. Your photos never leave your iPhone — we don't upload, store, or have access to your media. You can read more in our Privacy Policy.

Can I undo a mistake?

Absolutely. If you archive a photo by accident, just tap undo to bring it back. Nothing is permanently removed until you confirm deletion, so you're always in control.

Does archiving a photo delete it?

Not right away. Archiving moves a photo into your archive list. Photos are only removed from your library when you choose to clear the archive and confirm deletion.

What are streaks and daily challenges?

To make cleanup a habit, flicpix lets you build a daily streak by sorting a set number of photos each day. You can turn reminders on or off anytime in the app's notification settings.

How do I free up storage?

As you swipe and archive photos you no longer need, flicpix tracks how much space you'll reclaim. Tap to clear your archive and confirm, and that storage is freed up on your device.
